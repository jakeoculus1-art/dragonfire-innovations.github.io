**Privacy Policy for MediMate** Last updated: March 2026 MediMate ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how the MediMate mobile application handles information when you use the app. ## Information We Collect MediMate may collect and store the following information to provide its core functionality: **Account Information** When you create an account, MediMate uses Firebase Authentication to securely manage your login credentials such as your email address. **Medication Data** Information you enter into the app such as medication names, dosage schedules, reminders, and related notes is stored securely in Firebase Firestore in order to provide medication tracking and reminder features. **Device Notifications** The app uses notification permissions to remind you when medications are due. ## How We Use Information The information collected is used solely to: * Provide medication reminder functionality * Store your medication schedule * Send reminder notifications * Maintain your account login We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. ## Third-Party Services MediMate uses trusted third-party services to operate: * **Firebase Authentication** – for secure login * **Firebase Firestore** – to store medication data * **Firebase Cloud Messaging / Notifications** – to send reminder notifications These services may process data according to their own privacy policies. ## Data Security We take reasonable steps to protect your information using secure cloud services provided by Google Firebase. ## Your Control You may delete your medication data at any time by removing entries within the app. You may also delete your account. ## Children’s Privacy MediMate is not intended for children under the age of 13. ## Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.
